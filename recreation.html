Nahargarh Fort Cycling Expedition


A cycling expedition in a beautiful Pink morning of the city will lead one to Nahargarh. This expedition is a major boost in the lives of the participants as it helps them to explore the fort in a very new manner by checking the details that one easily misses. The trail is little quirky due to winding roads but the view of Jaipur city and Man Sagar Lake on its way is a worth. [1]


Jhalana Safari

Jaipur is known for its diverse wildlife that is found here that includes tigers, leopards, cheetahs, elephants and many other exotic species of animals and birds. If you are in Jaipur, then you should try taking a safari through Jhalana Wildlife Safari. Jhalana Leopard Safari tour in Jaipur is the perfect spot for spotting leopards and cheetahs.[1]



Nahargarh Biological Park

Nahargarh biological park is another best place in Jaipur for nature lovers. A wide variety of flora and fauna with land spam of about 7.25 sq Km make it a great visit near the outskirts of Jaipur. Around 250 species of birds, animals, reptiles, insects can be found here. The place is a famous attraction due to wildlife like tiger, lion, wild boar, chikara, crocodiles, Bengal Tiger, panthers, hyenas, wolves, black beer present here. Besides, the jungle safari is something you must not miss if you visit here. [1]


Hot Air Ballon Ride

This is one of the most amazing things to do in Jaipur, if you are an adventure enthusiast. Located near the Jamnalal Bajaj Road, Hot Air Balloon safari is conducted by various operators around the city and is a great way to beat the city blues. [1]
